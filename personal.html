personal projects

this is the first project i made when i decided i wanted to become a visual effects artist. i took inspiration from alyssaamalfoyy on tiktok, and i wanted to show my love for films using my favorite characters from some of my favorite shows and movies. i made this using a green screen and after effects. this is probably my favorite thing i've made so far.

for this piece, i experimented with sky replacement on adobe after effects. i enjoyed making this because i got to use a few different camera angles as well.
